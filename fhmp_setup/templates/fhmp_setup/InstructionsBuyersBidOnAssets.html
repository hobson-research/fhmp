All buyers see each seller's Reported Range and the fact checker's grade.

Next, all buyers bid on the sellers' assets. The buyer can bid on as many assets as they want.

For each asset, the buyer that bids the highest amount of points wins that asset. The winning buyer pays the points they bid to the seller and receives the asset.

Buyers that bid below the winning bid or do not bid do not win the asset and do not pay anything.
