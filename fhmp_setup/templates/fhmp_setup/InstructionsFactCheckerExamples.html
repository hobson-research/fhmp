Here is an example of how the Fact Checker works. For all three examples below, assume that:
(1) the asset True Value is 12 (but remember, this is unknown until after all bids are made)
(2) the Private Range (given to the seller only) is 12-14
(3) the Fact Checker’s range is 11-15
Please note that these are just examples, and the numbers we picked are not more or less likely than any other numbers we could have picked.

Example 1

  • The seller gives a Reported Range of 7-11
  • The Fact Checker’s grade is C, because the Fact Checker’s Range and the Reported Range over overlap a little.

Example 2

  • The seller gives a Reported Range of 11-15
  • The Fact Checker’s grade is A, because the Fact Checker’s Range and the Reported Range over overlap a lot.

Example 3

  • The seller gives a Reported Range of 16-20
  • The Fact Checker’s grade is F, because the Fact Checker’s Range and the Reported Range do not overlap at all.
